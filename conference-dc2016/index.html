Getting to Know PCORI: From Application to Closeout

January 28–29, 2016

Washington, DC

 

About PCORI

The Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI) is an independent, non-profit organization authorized by Congress in 2010. Its mission is to fund research that will provide patients, their caregivers and clinicians with the evidence-based information needed to make better-informed health care decisions. PCORI is committed to continuously seeking input from a broad range of stakeholders to guide its work. More information is available at www.pcori.org.

One-on-one Meetings with PCORI Staff

Staff will be available on Thursday and Friday for one-on-one sessions to answer any administrative or technical questions. For those who are completing applications in response to the upcoming Cycle 3 funding opportunities at PCORI, we can review your online application, perform a cursory budget review, and ensure that your application is assembled correctly.

Sign up here for a 15 minute one-on-one session to meet with a member from our staff. These meetings are available on a first-come first-serve basis, so sign up as soon as possible! If you have questions during the workshop, email contractsworkshop@pcori.org, and we will make sure to get you an answer.
