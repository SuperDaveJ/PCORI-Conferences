Getting to Know PCORI: From Application to Closeout

September 19-20, 2016

Boston, MA

 

About PCORI

The Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI) is an independent, non-profit organization authorized by Congress in 2010. Its mission is to fund research that will provide patients, their caregivers and clinicians with the evidence-based information needed to make better-informed health care decisions. PCORI is committed to continuously seeking input from a broad range of stakeholders to guide its work. More information is available at www.pcori.org.

As you exit the Sumner Tunnel, veer right into the tunnel for Storrow Drive. As you exit this tunnel, make a left following signs for “Leverett Circle / North Station."     Logan International Airport is just three miles from the Royal Sonesta Hotel.

    • Taxi service is available to and from Logan International Airport.
    • Travel from the airport to the Royal Sonesta Hotel is provided by Boston taxi companies. Fares are a metered rate and may range from $25–$30 depending upon traffic conditions and the time of arrival.
    • Return travel from the hotel back to the airport is provided by Cambridge taxi companies. Fares are flat rates, inclusive of all tolls, taxes and driver gratuities.

      • Flat rates are structured as follows:

    • Rush Hour (weekdays only): 7:00 a.m.–9:00 a.m. and 3:30 p.m.–6:30 p.m. $30.00 per taxi (1–4 passengers)
    • All other times, including weekends: $26.25 per taxi (1–4 passengers), mini vans and station wagons are slightly more (subject to change).
